The transformation formula is given by\-: \[ \Large \left \{ \begin{array}{r c l l l l l} x' & = & (a \times x) & + & (b \times y) & + & tx \\ y' & = & (c \times x) & + & (d \times y) & + & ty \end{array} \right . \] where x and y are the coordinates of any point, x' and y' the coordinates of the transformed point, tx and ty the horizontal and vertical components of the translation and where a, b, c and d are the coefficients of the matrix associated to the orientation.
